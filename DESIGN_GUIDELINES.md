# Legend of Darkness Project Design Guidelines

## Core Visual Style
1. **Perspective**: STRICTLY Isometric 2.5D (Diagonal View). All characters, monsters, items, buildings, and map tiles must be rendered in a standard isometric projection (usually 2:1 pixel ratio).
2. **Theme**: 90s Retro Dark Fantasy.
   - Mood: Gloomy, medieval European, heavy atmosphere.
   - Lighting: Warm yellow lights (candles, torches) contrast against dark blue/grey environments. Avoid sci-fi colors like neon purple unless specific magic.

## Asset Instructions
- **Characters**: 2-Head SD style. 5 visual tiers per class.
- **Buildings**: Isometric projection. Dark stone/wood textures. Yellow window lights.
- **Tiles**: Seamless isometric diamond tiles.
